Booking & Requirements

How do I book a car?

Simply download the GlideX app, browse the list of available cars in Kenya, choose your preferred vehicle, and confirm your booking instantly. Our platform is designed for a quick and seamless experience with no unnecessary waiting. Rent from Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, and other cities across Kenya.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You will need a valid national ID or passport, and a valid driver's license that you have held for at least one year. For foreigners, an International Driving Permit (IDP) may also be required. All documents must be valid and current to rent a car in Kenya.

What is the minimum age to rent a car?

The minimum age to rent a car on our platform is 23 years old. You must also have a valid driver's license held for at least one year. This policy ensures safe and reliable car rentals across Kenya.

Can tourists or foreigners rent cars on GlideX?

Yes. Visitors can rent cars on GlideX by providing a passport and a valid international or foreign driver’s license. Chauffeur service is also available for tourists who prefer not to drive themselves.

Can I rent a car with a driver or chauffeur in Nairobi or Mombasa?

Yes. GlideX gives you the option to rent cars with or without a chauffeur. You can select “Chauffeur Service” while booking to get a professional, verified driver for your trip — ideal for visitors or business travelers.

Can I rent a car in one city and drop it off in another?

Yes, selected hosts allow intercity drop-offs — for example, Nairobi to Mombasa. Look for the “Flexible Return” badge on listings or contact the host directly through the app to confirm this option.

Does GlideX support long-term car rentals?

Yes. GlideX allows daily, weekly, or monthly bookings. Long-term rentals often come with host discounts and priority customer support, making it ideal for business travelers or extended stays.

Can I use GlideX for business or corporate car hire?

Yes. GlideX supports corporate accounts for businesses that require cars for clients or employees. You can schedule multiple vehicles, request monthly invoices, and assign drivers for your organization.

Payments & Refunds

How much does it cost to rent a car in Kenya with GlideX?

GlideX offers flexible pricing based on the type of vehicle, location, and rental duration. Prices start from as low as KES 2,500 per day, with options for self-drive or chauffeur services. You can view live rates and host-set discounts directly in the app before booking.

Is insurance included in the rental price?

Yes, all cars listed on GlideX come with comprehensive insurance coverage. However, a security deposit is required and is fully refundable upon the safe return of the vehicle. This ensures protection for both renters and car hosts.

How do payments work on GlideX?

Payments are made securely through M-Pesa, PayPal, or card, and must be completed in full when booking. GlideX processes payments instantly and deducts a small 10% service commission from the host’s earnings.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking directly through the GlideX app. Our refund policy depends on the timing of your cancellation. Please refer to our terms and conditions for full details on the cancellation policy and how to request a refund.

How do refunds work if I cancel a booking?

Refunds depend on timing:
More than 48 hours before pickup → 80% refund
24–48 hours before → 50% refund
Less than 24 hours → No refund
Refunds are processed directly to your original payment method.

Hosts & Safety

Are GlideX hosts verified before listing their cars?

Absolutely. Every host and vehicle on GlideX goes through a verification process, which includes ID checks, logbook validation, and insurance review. This ensures your rental experience is safe and reliable.

What happens if I damage the car during my rental?

If damage occurs, your rental’s comprehensive insurance and security deposit help cover the repair costs. GlideX mediates between the renter and host to ensure a fair resolution based on verified inspection reports.

How does GlideX ensure my safety during rentals?

All transactions are cashless and cars are tracked using Apple AirTag or GPS integrations. In-app messaging keeps communication secure, and both renters and hosts are verified with national IDs or passports.

Can I list my car on GlideX and earn income?

Yes! Car owners can sign up as hosts on GlideX, upload car details, set their own prices, and start earning immediately. Payments are automated, and you can track your income through your Earnings Dashboard in the app.
